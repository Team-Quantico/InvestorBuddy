SAVING

INVESTING

When you invest your wealth add up EXPONENTIALLY.
On the other hand, Investing helps you to beat INFLATION.

Consider this …
If you deposited ₹1,00,000 in a savings account at 3 percent annual interest, it would grow to ₹1,82,075 in 20 years (before taxes). The same ₹1,00,000 invested in a stock mutual fund earning an average 10 percent a year would grow to ₹6,72,750 in 20 years (before taxes).

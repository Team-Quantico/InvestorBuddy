ADVICE: Before investing save a decent amount, ATLEAST 5 TIMES YOUR SALARY, in your savings account as EMERGENCY FUNDS.

STEP-1: You can achieve this by saving 40% of your salary every month for 1 year.

*Assuming that your bank gives 4% annual interest on savings account.

ADVICE: Now as you have saved your Emergency Funds, its time to take RISKS.
Maintain a 40:60 ratio between INVESTMENTS and EXPENSES
.Use that 60% of your salary for day-to-day expenses or monthly bills or EMIs.
And invest the remaining 40% of salary to meet your LIFE GOALS and to enjoy RETIREMENT.

STEP-2: Put 15% of your investing capital which is 6% of your salary in SAVINGS ACCOUNT every month to meet SHORT-TERM GOALS.

STEP-3: Put 70% of your investing capital which is 28% of your salary in a Systematic Investment Plan(SIP) every month to meet MID-TERM or LONG-TERM GOALS.

STEP-4: Put 70% of your investing capital which is 28% of your salary in National Pension Schemes every month to meet RETIREMENT GOALS.
